Installation:

To install simply place the @mod folder into your Arma 3 folder.
	This will most likely be located in your C:\ProgramFiles(x86)\steam\steamapps\common\Arma 3 directory

When you load up Arma 3 simply add the new mod to your Arma 3 launcher by clicking mods on the left
If you cannot find the mod listed in the menu to the right, simply click LOCAL MOD+.
Then add the @mod folder.
Click play and join DP or any of the current OFFICIAL servers in the community!
